Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Information Collection

InviteX Pro collects information you provide directly to us when you create an account, including your name, email, and profile picture. We also store messages and invitations you create to provide the app's functionality.

2. Use of Firebase

We use Google Firebase for authentication, database (Firestore), and storage. Your data is stored securely on Google's servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared with other users only when you intentionally follow them, send them invitations, or message them.

4. Security

We implement security measures including Firebase Security Rules to ensure that only authorized users can read or write their specific data.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@invitex.pro
